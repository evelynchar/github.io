About Me

Hello! I'm Charying Lam, a writer, art critic, and cultural commentator based in Hong Kong. My work explores the intersections of art, culture, and society, with a particular focus on fandom culture and socio-political issues. I have contributed to various platforms and my analysis often delves into the deeper implications of cultural phenomena.
